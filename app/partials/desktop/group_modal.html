Organize your workspaces with folders
Categorize your groups and DMs as you see fit.

Everyone has their own organizational strategy. Telegram shouldn't be limited to a single flowing list of conversations fighting for the top.

Telefuel provides a way to manage the chaos with folders.

In this demo, we bucket our conversations into Groups and Direct Messages, but you can organize your conversations however you want. Imagine categorizing your conversations based on different language communities, development channels, and community feedback. The possibilities are endless!
